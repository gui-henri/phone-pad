Try pressing the buttons!

PhonePad is an App that turns your phone into a full PC con- troller. It’s lightweight, wireless, and works on any game.

It aims to be not only a new way of playing old games, but also serve as base for new games.

We believe that new controllers are a key factor in innovation, and we want to provide a universal tool that can help with that.

PhonePad Pillars

What will drive the development.

PhonePad is based on two apps, one is in your PC and is responsible for RECEIVING the inputs and emulating a full controller.

The second part is in your phone, catching your inputs and SENDING them to your PC.
